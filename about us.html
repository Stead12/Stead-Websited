STEAD SOCIETY
(Science, Technology, Engineering and Arts Development Society) is a new organization within students at Fourah Bay College and Njala University that is taking an innovative approach to support minorities in STEAM fields and also promote Human Capital Development in Sierra Leone. The mission of STEAD is in TWO bigger fold:
  • To foster interaction and collaboration within students that are passionate about STEAM
  • To use innovative and creative ideas to provoke thought discussions and engagement of issues affecting students, minorities in STEAM and the country

We plan to achieve these goals by advocating for more practical knowledge in the STEAM field and also train the young generation to identify and master their profession for the benefit of the nation and promote human capital development in Sierra Leone. The above goals comprises of greater efforts from STEAD to foster communication within the STEAM community and the greater public, and in doing so, help to bring a voice to students and minorities facing challenges in the STEAM field and also to invest in human capital development.

OBJECTIVES FOR THIS STEAD LAB

  • “STEAD” Recommend, Sensitize, train and educate students and youths that are passionate about STEAD to take part in providing educative and innovative solutions to solve pressing life challenges in their School, College, Community and their nation as a whole.
  • ”STEAD” discourages any form of discrimination that will perpetrate among ethnics, region, disabilities, and political cause
  • ”STEAD” we help the government in fulfilling their core goals. That is, quality education and human capital development by offering our service in terms of mentorship, tutorials and projects for school going children, university students and youth across the country in science, Technology and Innovation.
  • “STEAD” planned to be the bridge that connects the nation or institutions to powerful and sustainable nations with developmental partners who have made their names by providing solution to the world through STEAD programs. We will also partner with organizations in promoting growth in the mentality of students and youth.
  • “STEAD” promise to promote and advocate for students and youth by ensuring that the get fair treatment from communities residing and integrate all students by organizing Science, Technology, engineering and Art fair by participating in different extracurricular activities like Space programs, Educational events and recreational programs amongst different learning fields in the nation.
  • “STEAD” assist in the personal development of youths through both formal and practical training sessions and daily interaction in the areas of leadership, Science, Technology, Engineering, moral and values, clarification, social responsibility, programming and interpersonal cooperation.
