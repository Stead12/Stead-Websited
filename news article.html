Members of STEAD were certified and recognized by Deen of Engineering

STEAD Society were invited to an enterpreneurs workshop, which was held at CIvil Building Fourah Bay College

This hospicious and incredibly educative event was hosted by the Freetown Business School, Sierra Leone Institution of Engineers, OC Associates, Engineers for change in Sierra Leone and Royal Academy of Engineering

STEAD was invited based on the fact that, STEAD promotes development through engineering and innovation and indeed STEAD delivered exactly on what the organisation promised by an amazing performance that was acknowledged by the designated tutor Dr Peter Fearon

Members of STEAD contributed to the event by sharing their incredible business plan, which according to the organisation aim at promoting innovaton and most importantly to support the circular economy of the country
